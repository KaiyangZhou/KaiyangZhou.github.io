About Me

I am currently a postdoc at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, working with Prof. Ziwei Liu and Prof. Chen Change Loy. I received my PhD in 2020 from the University of Surrey, UK, under the supervision of Prof. Tao Xiang. See my profile for more.

I am on the job market. Feel free to reach out. :)

Research Interests

I am generally interested in AI, machine learning and computer vision. I aim to develop AI that is i) generalizable, having the ability to generalize to unseen domains and scenarios; ii) adaptable, meaning that the agent can adapt to new environments in an efficient manner; iii) transferable, such that the acquired knowledge can be transferred to new tasks or problems. I am particularly interested in the following topics:

  • domain generalization, domain adaptation, transfer learning
  • semi-supervised learning, X-shot learning (X=zero/few), unsupervised learning

If you are looking for the codes, models or data associated with my papers, please see the Publications page.
