About Me

I am currently a postdoc at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, working with Prof. Ziwei Liu and Prof. Chen Change Loy. I received my PhD in 2020 from the University of Surrey, UK, under the supervision of Prof. Tao Xiang. See my profile for more.

Research Interests

I am generally interested in AI and machine learning. I aim to develop AI that is 1) generalizable, for being able to generalize to unseen domains and scenarios; 2) adaptable, meaning that the agent can efficiently adapt to new environments with some additional, but easy-to-obtain, new information; 3) transferable, such that the learned knowledge can be easily transferred to new tasks or problems. I am particularly interested in the following topics:

  • domain generalization, domain adaptation, transfer learning
  • semi-supervised learning, X-shot learning (X=zero/few), unsupervised learning
