About Me

I am currently a postdoc at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, working with Chen Change Loy and Ziwei Liu. I received my PhD (2020) from the University of Surrey, UK, under the supervision of Tao Xiang. I work on computer vision and machine learning.

Research Interests

  • Visual recognition with deep learning
  • Transfer learning, domain adaptation, and domain generalization
