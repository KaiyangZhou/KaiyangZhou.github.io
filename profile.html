Bio

Kaiyang Zhou is currently a postdoctoral researcher at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, working with Ziwei Liu and Chen Change Loy. He obtained his PhD (2020) from the University of Surrey, UK, under the supervision of Tao Xiang. Prior to that, he received his MSc with Distinction (2016) from the University of Bristol, UK; his BSc with 1st class honor (2015) from Ulster University, UK; and his BEng (2015) from Fujian Normal University, China. He has worked in Samsung AI Center Cambridge, UK, as a research intern from 2018–19, and in Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences, as a research assistant with Yu Qiao from 2016–17.

Kaiyang's research interests generally lie in computer vision and machine learning. His research centers around the development of robust, generalizable and data-efficient learning algorithms and their applications in computer vision. His work has been published in top-tier venues, such as TPAMI, ICCV and ICLR. He serves as a reviewer for international journals and conferences, e.g., TPAMI, CVPR and ICCV. He is also an active contributor to the open-source community (representative projects are Torchreid and Dassl.pytorch).
