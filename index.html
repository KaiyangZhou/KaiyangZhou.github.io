About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London, supervised by Tao Xiang and Andrea Cavallaro. My research interests are computer vision, machine learning and deep learning.

Prior to my PhD study, I was doing research with Yu Qiao on video summarization in Multimedia Research Center, SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I hold an MSc (Distinction) in Machine Learning from University of Bristol and a BSc (First Class) in Computer Science from University of Ulster.
