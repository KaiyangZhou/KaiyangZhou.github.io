Profile

Short Bio (CV)

Dr. Kaiyang Zhou is currently a research fellow (affiliated with MMLab@NTU) at NTU Singapore, working with Prof. Ziwei Liu and Prof. Chen Change Loy. He received his PhD (2020) in computer science from the University of Surrey, UK, under the supervision of Prof. Tao Xiang. His research lies at the intersection of machine learning and computer vision, with specialization in domain generalization, transfer learning, and efficient deep learning. His papers have been published at major journals and conferences in relevant fields, such as Trans. PAMI, IJCV and CVPR, and have been cited more than 1,800 times with h-index at 14 (as of Sep 2022). He is a guest editor of IJCV, and has served as an area chair/senior program committee member for BMVC'22 and AAAI'23. He is also an active contributor to the open-source community on Github (representative projects are Torchreid and Dassl.pytorch).
